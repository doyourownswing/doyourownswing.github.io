Thursday Night Classes and Social

Elevate Your Dancing

DYOS offers four classes of different levels, which cover topics ranging from the basics, to exploring the creative opportunities within West Coast Swing. Join us for one or more classes, no partner required. Not sure which class is for you? Shoot us a message! Stay for social dancing starting at 9:15pm!

Masking Policy

DYOS's health policy is aimed at mitigating the spread of Covid-19 and reducing the severity of breakthrough infections.

  • X94+ Masks (KN95, N95, or KF94 with no exhaust port) are required for classes

  • X94+ Masks MAY be required for social dancing, depending on prevalence of COVID-19 in Santa Clara County

KN95 masks are available for sale at check-in - $1 cash / Zelle; $2 Venmo / PayPal

1

Level 1

West Coast Swing Foundations
Sky Ballroom (second floor)
8:30pm - 9:15pm

For those who are brand new to West Coast Swing or developing a basic pattern vocabulary.

Prerequisites

Come as you are! No partner or experience required!

2

Level 2

Beyond the Basics
Spotlight Ballroom
7:30pm - 8:00pm

Learn beginner+ and intermediate patterns with a focus on repetition and developing comfort with each pattern.

Prerequisites

For those who have attended at least one introductory level course at DYOS or another studio/event.

3

Level 3

Skills and Drills
Spotlight Ballroom
8:05pm - 8:35pm

This class will introduce you to movement, connection, and rhythm theories, skills, and methods. It will be a time to focus on skills via drills. This class will have less music and actual dancing than the average dance class, but will develop awareness and intention in your approach to West Coast Swing!

Prerequisites

You should know how to do the following:

  • Tuck Turn (sugar and/or passing)

  • Spinning Side Pass (inside roll and/or free spin)

  • Starter Step

  • 8-Count Whip

4

Level 4

Artistic Application
Spotlight Ballroom
8:40pm - 9:10pm

Learn the skills for improvised, partnered musical expression with 2-3 applications of Level 3 Class material. The focus of this class will be responding to the music and your partner! This class will help you cultivate those magical "How can this dance be improvised??" moments.

Prerequisites

All of the Skills and Drills (Level 3) prerequisites, as well as general comfort navigating social dances
